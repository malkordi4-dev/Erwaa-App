# 💧 Erwaa (إرواء) - Water Crisis Management System in Gaza

An integrated digital system and smart application designed to optimize water distribution management, minimize water loss, and provide real-time data to mitigate water scarcity challenges and enhance water security in the Gaza Strip.

---

## 📋 Project Overview
* **Institution:** University College of Applied Sciences (UCAS)
* **Department:** Information Technology
* **Academic Year:** 2026

---

## 🚀 Project Motto
> **"Fair distribution, monitored pricing, and secure access for every citizen."**

---

## 📊 Context & Baseline Indicators
The **Erwaa** platform was developed as an urgent, tech-driven response based on critical statistical indicators reflecting the severe water crisis in Gaza:
* **93%** of households face direct water insecurity.
* **15 Liters/Capita** is the minimum water available for survival (well below international standards).
* **40%** efficiency rate of current drinking water facilities, demanding immediate digital intervention to raise operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Key Features & Strategic Objectives
* **For Consumers:** User-friendly UI/UX to request water, track orders in real-time, and significantly reduce waiting times.
* **For Suppliers & Authorities:** A smart priority-based water distribution framework, network monitoring, and price control management.
* **For Decision Makers:** Generating accurate reports and live statistics on water consumption and distribution for effective crisis management.

---

## 🛠️ Agile - Scrum Methodology
The project adopts the **Agile - Scrum** framework to ensure maximum flexibility and adaptability in crisis management through:
1. **Iterative Delivery (Sprints):** Breaking down development into 2-to-3-week cycles.
2. **Continuous Quality:** Performing continuous Unit and Integration testing at the end of each sprint.
3. **Execution Phases:** 
   * Requirements Gathering & System Analysis
   * UI/UX Design (Figma)
   * Frontend & Backend Development
   * Quality Assurance Testing & Final Deployment

---

## 🎨 Visual Identity & UX Standards
* **Color Palette:** Professional aquatic tones (Sky Blue, Dark Blue, Aqua Green, and Turquoise) representing purity and flow.
* **Accessibility:** Highly simplified interfaces tailored to ensure ease of use for all segments of society during crisis situations.
* **RTL Support:** Full Right-to-Left alignment for a seamless and premium Arabic user experience.
